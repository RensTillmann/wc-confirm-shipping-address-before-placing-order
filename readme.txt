Let customers double check their shipping address before placing their order on checkout page. Helps to prevent incorrectly entered shipping addresses

== Description ==

A simple plugin specifically designed to work with WooCommerce.

Upon checkout the customer will be presented with a popup/modal where they require to confirm or review their shipping address they entered. 

Only if they confirm their shipping address they will be able to finalize the order and proceed with the payment.
